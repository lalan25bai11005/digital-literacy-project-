## **Social Media for College Students: The Essentials**





### ​✅ Do's



​ **- Keep your profile professional.** Think of your bio and profile picture as your first digital handshake.



​ **- Lock down your privacy settings.** Take ten minutes to make sure only your actual friends can see your tagged photos.



​ **- Fact-check before sharing information.** Don’t be the person accidentally spreading fake campus news or misinformation.



​ **- Be respectful in discussions.** Even if you disagree, keeping it respectful prevents a digital argument from haunting you later.



​ **- Think before posting.** If you’re tired, angry, or "celebrating" a bit too hard, put the phone down for an hour.



### **​❌ Don'ts**



​ **- Don't post your exact location.** Avoid sharing your dorm room number or your specific daily class schedule.



​ **- Don't get baited into online drama.** No one ever "wins" an argument in a comment section; it just makes everyone look bad.



​ **- Don't Share personal information publicly.** It involves Safety RSisks, Identity Theft, and Digital Stalking'



​ **- Don't use offensive language.** A joke that feels funny in a group chat can look like a disaster when screenshotted and taken out of context.



​ **- Don't click every link in your DMs.** College students are prime targets for phishing scams offering "easy remote jobs" or "free gift cards."
